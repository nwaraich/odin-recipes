Spicy Tuna Salad


Ingredients

  • 1 Canned Tuna
  • 1 tablespoon Japanese Mayo
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha, add as needed for spice level
  • 1/4 cup cucumber
  • 1/4 cup celery

Instructions

  1. Mix Mayo and siracha until desired flavour and spice reached, set aside
  2. cut cucumer, celery and mix together with tuna
  3. put in sauce and mix everything together
  4. serve with desired method
